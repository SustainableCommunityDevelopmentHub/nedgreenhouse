Community Greenhouse Build Process

During the buid process, The process of building this greenhouse is being done weekly on saturdays with volunteers from the mountain community. The steps are being shared here online and participants are encouraged to come ready to work, with warm clothes, an open mind, and willingness to meet new friends.
