Completed Steps Winter 2018

0. Acquire & debark timberframing logs.


1. Dig foundation 4 feed deep. Our dimentions are 17'4" by 10'4" and a 16" wall.


2. Fill in entire area with pea gravel to 1 foot below soil level.


3. Tamp with electric tamp or by hand to level


4. Stack large rubble pieces in a flat layer with mortar between pieces. Stack continous layers with mortar between pieces that intersect with midpoint of the first layer pieces until level with soil.
